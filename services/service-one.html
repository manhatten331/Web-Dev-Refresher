Web Devolopment Experience

I remember the first day I started the bootcamp, I think I was more excited to meet people who wanted to code like me! Of course apart of me was nervous but at that time my back was against the wall and I was willing to do everything possible to make sure I got something out of it.

My time before I got my fist job was very humbling experience my days were mostly the same but it taught me discpline. In chronological order I would code for 3-4 hours, get food, apply to as many jobs as I could, and attend networking events if they were available. What I miss most about that time is the networking. I loved to meet people in the industry tell them my story and usually they would offer some advice that ended helping me land my first job!
